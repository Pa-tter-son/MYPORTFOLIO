About me

The story
behind the work

I'm Patterson — a creative technologist based in Accra, Ghana, with a background spanning biomedical engineering, customer and client experience, front-end development, education, and music.

My journey into web development is driven by the belief that great design and clean code are two sides of the same coin. I approach every project with the same energy I bring to music direction and my engagement with clients and customers — intentional, collaborative, and focused on impact.

Beyond screens, I've worked in medical equipment operations, financial administration, and youth governance — giving me a rare combination of technical depth and human-centered thinking.

Currently levelling up in JavaScript and React while building KasaFrames — a project close to my heart.
